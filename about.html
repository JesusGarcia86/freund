Andrew Freund

Andrew Freund has been an on-air television host, journalist and film critic for over 15 years. He can be seen weekly on the nationally syndicated FOX television program Dish Nation, talking all things film and television with today’s most celebrated entertainment icons. He has a been a frequent guest on FOX LA, 'The Talk,' 'Wendy Williams,' Canada’s CTV, SiriusXM and more as a Hollywood Insider. After years of covering the Oscars, Golden Globes, Grammys and Emmys he is a much sought after pop culture expert.


A Northern California Native, Andrew’s love of movies began when he was 6 years old after seeing 'Willy Wonka and the Chocolate Factory.' His love of film brought him to film school at UC Santa Cruz where he made student and documentary films. When he is not working, he can be found at crossfit, swimming, exploring and trying new restaurants. As an avid traveler he has been to more that 25 countries and over 6 continents. Andrew is an active member of the Critics Choice Association and resides in Los Angeles.
